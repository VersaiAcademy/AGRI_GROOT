Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

At VIKJAN GROOT AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED, we strive to deliver your orders promptly and in perfect condition. Please review our shipping and delivery policies below.

1. Shipping Partners & Timeframes

We partner with leading logistics providers to ensure reliable delivery:

  • Standard Shipping: 6-12 working days via Delhivery, FedEx, Xpressbees, Gati, etc.
  • Express Shipping: Available at additional cost (3-5 working days)
  • Bulk Orders: 48-72 hours processing time before dispatch
Note: Delivery to remote areas may take longer. Some locations may require self-collection from nearest courier hub.

2. Order Processing

Our order fulfillment process:

  • New customers will receive order confirmation call for address verification
  • Orders processed within 24-48 hours (excluding weekends/holidays)
  • You'll receive tracking information via SMS/email once shipped

3. Delivery Inspection

For your protection:

  • Inspect packaging before accepting delivery
  • Refuse damaged parcels and notify us immediately
  • Take photos of any visible damage for documentation
  • Email evidence to support@vikjangroot.com with order details

4. Delivery Limitations

Important considerations:

  • Estimated delivery dates are not guaranteed
  • Weather conditions may affect delivery timelines
  • Non-serviceable PIN codes will require alternate delivery address
  • ODA (Out of Delivery Area) locations require self-collection
Important: VIKJAN GROOT is not liable for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances including but not limited to natural disasters, transportation strikes, or customs delays.

5. Free Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on:

  • All orders above $500 (after discounts)
  • Selected promotional items (as specified)
  • Loyalty program members (Gold tier and above)
