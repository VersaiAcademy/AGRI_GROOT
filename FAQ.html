Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

At VIKJAN GROOT AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED,your satisfaction is our top priority. Please review the following FAQs to understand our cancellation and refund process clearly.

Ordering & Payments

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by selecting the products you want, adding them to your cart, and proceeding through the checkout process. You'll need to provide shipping information and payment details to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), net banking, UPI payments, and cash on delivery (for select areas).

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Yes, we use secure SSL encryption for all transactions. Your payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways, and we do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 12 hours of placement, provided it hasn't been processed for shipment. For orders already in processing, please contact our customer support immediately.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your delivery areas and timelines?

We deliver across India with varying timelines:

  • Metro cities: 2-3 business days
  • Other urban areas: 3-5 business days
  • Rural areas: 5-7 business days
Exact delivery times will be shown during checkout based on your location.

Do you offer express delivery?

Yes, we offer express delivery for select products and locations at an additional charge. This service guarantees delivery within 24-48 hours where available.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email/SMS. You can track your order by clicking the tracking link in your email or by logging into your account on our website.

What should I do if my order is delayed?

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances. If your order is significantly delayed, please contact our customer support for assistance.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for most products, provided they are in original condition with packaging intact. Perishable items and opened products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support with your order number and reason for return. We'll guide you through the return process and provide a return authorization if eligible.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned items. The time taken for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your payment method and bank processing times.

What if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately with photos of the product and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Product & Quality

Are your agricultural products organic?

We offer both organic and conventionally grown products. Organic products are clearly labeled as such and come with appropriate certifications where applicable.

How do you ensure product quality?

We work directly with trusted farmers and suppliers, conduct regular quality checks, and follow strict storage and handling procedures to ensure our products meet high quality standards.

Do you offer bulk purchasing options?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for bulk order inquiries and customized solutions for your needs.

Can I get product recommendations for specific needs?

Absolutely! Our agricultural experts can provide personalized recommendations based on your specific requirements. Contact our customer support or use the chat feature on our website.
